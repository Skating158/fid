Adoptions

Welcome to the adoption center, in this page we provide all the information needed in order for you to adopt a pet of your very own. Our catalog varies and changes quite often as new animals are rescued and put up for adoption. The catalog feature many different pets to choose from, some you may not even expect to be seen for adoption.


Puppies

Here we have Alex, Beatrice, and Cynthia; three puppies found abandoned near the interstate.


Kittens

Meet Jack and Jill, two small kittens discovered in the aftermath of a forest fire.


Turkey

This is Tyrion the Turkey, he was found near the countryside, tangled up in a wired fence but made a full recovery.


Guinea Pig

Meet Peppa the Guinea Pig, she is a small Guinea Pig who was found foraging around near a wast dump, after a few baths she is nice and clean, ready to be taken in by a new family.
