Volunteers Welcome!

Welcome any and all future volunteers to the Pet Rescue Organization Volunteer page, in this page we'll provide all the qualifications and details needed in order for you to become an officially licensed rescue or trainer.

Courage

All of our volunteers must have a sense of hope and courage for the successful rescue and saving of an animal, we will never turn our backs on an animal in need.

Teamwork

Teamwork is the main tactic behind all of our rescue and rehabilitation operations, no one can do it all themselves, therefore everyone will contribute to the well-being of the rescued animal.

Love for animals

Here in PRO, we promote a strong love for animals of all kinds and will never mistreat them in any way despite of their behaviour. Therefore, we ask that any future volunteers have an excellent relationship and love for animals.

More Qualifications

  • Ability to multitask
  • Withstand stressful conditions
  • Ability to work on the rescue for long periods of time
  • Ages 17 and up

Background Check

As a volunteer-friendly organization we ask that all future volunteers interested provide us with a minimum background check in order to determine eligibility of their position. We will never disclose these background checks to anyone other than our human resources segment of the organization, we do not allow discrimination of any kind and will never condone harrassment in the workplace. If any issues involoving workers and animals rights arise please feel free to contact out immediate hotline, 789-PRO.
